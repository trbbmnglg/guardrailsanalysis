This tool utilizes CrewAI to orchestrate a team of specialized autonomous agents. Instead of a single model checking your work, six distinct personas analyze your prompt in parallel:

  • SEC Security Auditor: Validates OWASP LLM Top 10 & ISO 42001 compliance—prompt injection resilience, hardcoded secrets detection, unauthorized tool access prevention, and DoS protection.
  • PRV Privacy Officer: Enforces GDPR/CCPA/NIST AI RMF standards—PII leakage prevention, data minimization, ephemeral data handling, and mandatory self-validation mechanisms.
  • RAI Responsible AI Architect: Certifies EU AI Act & NIST AI RMF alignment—systemic bias mitigation, harmful content filtering, human-in-the-loop requirements, and explainability (XAI) mandates.
  • QA Quality Engineer: Validates ISO/IEC 25059 quality standards—functional suitability, output schema enforcement, adversarial resilience, and self-correction mechanisms.
  • COST Cost Architect: Analyzes computational complexity and assigns tier ratings (1-5)—estimates latency impact, resource overhead, and provides optimization recommendations for scalability.
